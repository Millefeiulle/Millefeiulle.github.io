Why Should I choose MediCore Medical Center

Our reputation for outstanding care and family-like atmosphere, together with advanced medical technology and facilities ensures we attract leading consultants and specialists from the industry to work with us. All our clinicians are board-certified and come with very high credentials within their field of speciality. Many of our consultant doctors are internationally recognised as well. Whilst practicing at our facilities, our consultants comply with Clinical Governance system that ensures adequate patient care. All our nurses are fully qualified and registered with the Nursing Council. At MediCore Medical Center we are committed to providing our patients with advanced medical and surgical treatments with access to the latest treatment technology available.

What are MediCore Medical Center's visiting hours?

Our visiting hours are from 8:30am to 10:30pm from Monday to Friday, whilst are ER department is open for visit at all times.

How many visitors are allowed?

Our Medical Center encourages relatives and friends to visit patients. But patients can get tired very quickly. For this reason, the number of visitors each patient is allowed is usually restricted, typically to no more than 2 people at any one time. It might be necessary to stagger the visitors so they come at different times. There can be restrictions on children visiting a patient. Please check the arrangements with the ward you're going to before your visit.

cardiology

Heart disease is one of the world's major health issues, and cardiovascular disease is the number one cause of death. We, at the MediCore Medical Clinic, aim to reduce the risks and lifestyle limitations associated with heart disease and vascular disease. We ensure providing international standards of care to patients. Our richly experienced doctors are capable of attending to all types of cardiac problems and meeting the requirements of each and every patient. The department facilities and treatment features include:

  • Evaluation of all cardiac problems.
  • Ischemic heart diseases (heart attacks).
  • Valvular heart diseases.

ophthalmology

At the Eye Care department, we have competent ophthalmologists trained and experienced to diagnose, treat and manage all the problems of the eyes and visual system. They medically manage all eye diseases and eye injuries, eye strain, dry eyes, and eye infections. Our skilled staff also diagnose and treat Refractive errors and Amblyopia. Besides this, they also treat the floowing:

  • Treatment of Myopia.
  • Diagnosis and treatment of Glaucoma (eye pressure).
  • Diagnosis of Diabetic Retinopathy, Hypertensive Retinopathy.

orthodontics

Taking proper care of your teeth and gums is a life long commitment. For that, regular visits to your dentist is necessary. The Dental Care Department boasts of all the subspecialties in Dentistry manned by more that one doctor. Uses latest and highly refined techniques for orthodontic corrections.
